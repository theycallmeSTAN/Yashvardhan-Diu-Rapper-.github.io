Yashvardhan — Independent Rap Artist

Biography

Yashvardhan is an independent hip hop artist originating from Diu Island, India, and currently based in Mumbai Bandra West. His work focuses on real-life storytelling, artistic growth, and guiding beginner rappers entering the hip-hop culture.

Through music and educational content, he documents the journey of building an independent career while learning from the Mumbai music ecosystem.
