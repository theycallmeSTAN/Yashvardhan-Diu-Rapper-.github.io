Diu to Mumbai: My Rap Journey

I started my music journey from Diu Island, a small coastal place where hip-hop culture is still growing. Like many independent artists, resources were limited but passion was strong.

Moving to Mumbai, especially Bandra West, exposed me to real artist struggles, networking, studio environments, and creative competition. This transition helped me understand that growth in music requires consistent learning and adaptation.

Through this platform, I document my journey to help beginner rappers avoid common mistakes and build confidence while entering hip-hop.
